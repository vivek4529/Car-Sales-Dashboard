# 🚗 Car Sales Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard built to analyze car sales performance across dealer regions, brands, colors, engine types, transmission types, body styles, and monthly trends. It highlights total sales, total orders, and average price from the sales dataset [file:1].

## 📌 Project Overview

This dashboard gives a quick visual summary of overall sales performance and product mix. It helps identify top-performing dealer regions, popular car brands, and seasonal sales patterns [file:1].

## ✨ Features

- 📊 KPI cards for **Total Sales**, **Total Orders**, and **Average Price** [file:1].
- 🗺️ Sales breakdown by **Dealer Region** [file:1].
- 🏷️ Sales by **Brand** [file:1].
- 🎨 Unit sold by **Color** [file:1].
- ⚙️ Unit sold by **Engine** and **Transmission** [file:1].
- 🚙 Unit sold by **Body Style** [file:1].
- 📈 Monthly sales trend chart [file:1].
- 🧾 Detailed sales table with dealer and vehicle information [file:1].

## 🧩 Dashboard Pages

### 1. Overview Page
Shows KPI cards, regional sales, brand performance, body style distribution, and donut charts for color, engine, and transmission splits [file:1].

### 2. Table Page
Contains detailed transaction-level data including dealer name, region, company, model, engine, transmission, color, body style, and price [file:1].

### 3. Monthly Trend Page
Displays sales by month to observe seasonal spikes and dips throughout the year [file:1].

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Open the `.pbix` file in **Power BI Desktop**.
2. Refresh the dataset if required.
3. Use the **Year** slicer to filter the dashboard.
4. Explore each visual to compare regions, brands, and monthly sales patterns [file:1].

## 💡 Insights

- **Austin**, **Janesville**, and **Scottsdale** appear among the stronger dealer regions [file:1].
- **Chevrolet**, **Ford**, and **Dodge** are among the top brands shown in the report [file:1].
- **SUV** and **Hatchback** are the leading body styles in the visualization [file:1].
- Sales rise sharply in the later months, especially near the end of the year [file:1].
